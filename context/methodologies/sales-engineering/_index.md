# Sales Engineering Methodology

This document outlines the context for sales engineering tasks.

## Key Terms
- **PoV (Proof of Value):** A pre-sales engagement where we demonstrate the value of our solution to a prospective customer.
- **Executive Readout Deck:** A slide presentation summarizing the results of a PoV for the customer's leadership team. It typically includes the initial challenges, our solution, the results, and proposed next steps.

## Core Workflows
- **Creating a PoV Readout Deck:** This is a common task at the conclusion of a PoV. The goal is to create a compelling presentation that justifies the customer's investment in our solution.
